Certified Kubernetes Administrator (CKA)

What is CKA?

The Certified Kubernetes Administrator (CKA) exam is designed to validate the skills required to be a successful Kubernetes administrator in industry roles. CKA ensures that the candidate can perform the responsibilities of Kubernetes administrators, including installation, configuration, and troubleshooting of a Kubernetes cluster.

Why Become Certified?

Kubernetes has become a standard for container orchestration, and certification validates your expertise in managing Kubernetes clusters in production environments. The CKA certification proves you have the skills to handle tasks such as application lifecycle management, networking, security, and troubleshooting.

CKA Exam Overview

  • Duration: 2 hours
  • Format: Online, proctored, performance-based tasks
  • Certification Validity: 3 years
  • Cost: $300 (subject to change)

Skills Tested

  • Cluster Installation and Configuration
  • Workloads and Scheduling
  • Services and Networking
  • Storage and Volume Management
  • Troubleshooting
